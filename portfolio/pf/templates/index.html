All About Mike!

Hello! Thanks for viewing my portfolio. My name is Michael Lopez, and I am an inspiring web developer. My love for web development came from the website Neopets, which allows you to edit your virtual pets home pages using HTML and CSS. Through designing web pages, I was inspired to go after web development as a career and enrolled in college. Through college, I learned about programming, using C++ at first but then afterwards learning Java, Ruby, Python and a few other languages.
I began to develop sites in my time after graduating with an associates in Computer Technology at SUNY Jefferson in Watertown, New York. I used Ruby-on-Rails for the backend of my sites and used HTML/CSS/JavaScript for the frontend. I was able to build a project for a friend for the very site that got me into web design, Neopets. Eventually, I went back to college as I decided to switch majors to Computer Science and graduated from SUNY Potsdam with my Bachelors at the end of 2020. I now prefer developing sites using Django for the backend and have worked on two projects utilizing it.
When I'm not writing code, I enjoy spending times outdoors, taking walks through forests or by lakes and watching the animals. I also enjoy cooking, playing video games and creating art, both traditionally and digitally. I love making pixel art the most, as I find it's a great way to relieve stress.
