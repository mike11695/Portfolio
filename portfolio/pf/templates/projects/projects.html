List.it

Website

List.it is a web application that I developed as my senior project in 2020 at SUNY Potsdam. The idea for the site came about after seeing my mom and aunt using Facebook Marketplace and groups to sell and auction off items that they owned. I felt that it was a quite bit difficult to use for them as they had to track bids and offers throughout different groups, which can quickly become confusing. I wanted to see if I could make an application that would be more efficient in helping users manage their items and know who to contact regarding items.
When developing the site, I also had a bit of inspiration from other web sites such as eBay, LetGo and Carousell. Users are able to upload pictures of their objects to the site and then create items using the photos. Using these created items, they can either add them to listings to trade or auction or use them to offer on other listings. Users are also allowed to create wishlists and wishlist listings to find certain items that may not currently be listed on site. Users will only be able to see listings in about a 30 mile radius of where they live, but they can use a search function to increase the search radius up to 50 miles.
List.it includes some additional features such as allowing users to communicate through the site, leave ratings, host events, make payments on site using PayPal and more. To read more about these features, I have included the report I had made for the project below.
